    The Right Moves

    At Baldwin Anka Sennecke Halman LLP, we help our clients make the right moves. We believe the best legal service is one that produces successful outcomes--reliably, promptly and cost-effectively. This is the guiding principle behind our firm.

    A team of seasoned law professionals created Baldwin Anka Sennecke Halman LLP in a form that works best for clients. Good credentials. Responsive size. Minimal management. Low overhead expenses. Solid referral network.

    Legal counsel plays a key role in each client's success. Baldwin Anka Sennecke Halman LLP accepts that responsibility in every aspect of legal service. We work closely with the owners and managers of both public and privately-owned companies. In our collective experience, we have concluded thousands of matters to our clients' satisfaction.

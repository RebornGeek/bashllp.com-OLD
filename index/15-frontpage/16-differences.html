Some Important Differences

Several factors distinguish Baldwin Anka Sennecke Halman LLP from other legal firms. First, we don't try to be all things to all people. Our practice is generally restricted to corporate and commercial law, securities, real estate, commercial litigation, estates, employment, immigration and family law. This focus helps us concentrate on each client's individual requirements.

Our focus provides a second benefit. It allows us to recommend specific counsel in other specialized areas such as tax law. We can give our clients the benefit of a contemporary trend in legal services: networking among specialty firms which best suit our clients' needs. Unlike large firms, we are not obliged to use another in-house department.

Third, our smaller size simplifies our management and lets us concentrate on client service. Lower overhead also takes the pressure off our fees. Smaller size, however, does not mean smaller talent. With a solid track record, we offer our clients the creative and experienced advice they need to succeed.

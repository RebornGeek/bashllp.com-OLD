Dylan T. White   -   Associate

Called to the Ontario Bar, 2009.

Dylan White practises corporate, commercial, securities and real estate law.

Dylan advises clients on a broad range of corporate/commercial matters, including incorporations, corporate governance, shareholder agreements, business acquisitions and general commercial matters.

In his securities practice Dylan advises clients on public and private offerings of securities, continuous disclosure and regulatory compliance matters, with an emphasis on investment funds. He also advises investment fund managers, portfolio managers and exempt market dealers on securities registration matters.

In his real estate practice Dylan acts for clients on buying, selling and financing residential and commercial properties, as well as drafting and negotiating commercial leases.
